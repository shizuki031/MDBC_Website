Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment? ▶
You can easily book an appointment through our online form on the Appointment page. Once submitted, our dental staff will contact you via email or phone to confirm your schedule.
Do you accept walk-in patients? ▶
Yes, we accommodate walk-in patients whenever possible. However, we highly recommend setting an appointment in advance to ensure you're attended to promptly.
What dental services do you offer? ▶
We offer general dentistry, orthodontics, teeth cleaning, restorations, extractions, cosmetic dentistry, veneers, and teeth whitening. Visit our Services page for full details.
How often should I visit the dentist? ▶
We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for routine cleaning and oral check-ups to maintain healthy teeth and gums.
Do you accept dental insurance or HMO? ▶
Yes, we accept select dental insurance and HMO providers. Please contact our staff for verification or bring your HMO card during your visit.
What should I do in case of a dental emergency? ▶
For emergencies such as severe tooth pain, bleeding, or broken teeth, contact us immediately through our clinic hotline. We accommodate urgent cases as much as possible.
