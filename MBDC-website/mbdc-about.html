About Maglinao-Banca Dental Clinic

Situated in the heart of Binangonan, Maglinao-Banca Dental Clinic is dedicated to providing high-quality, affordable, and compassionate dental care for the whole family. Since opening, our goal has been to create a friendly and comfortable environment where every patient feels valued and confident with their smile.

Mission

To deliver high-quality, essential dental care that is accessible and affordable to every member of our community, focusing on preventative health and compassionate treatment.

Vision

A community where cost is never a barrier to essential dental care, and where every person feels valued, educated, and completely comfortable during their visit.

Dr. Ernestina Maglinao - Banca

Resident Dentist & Founder

Dr. Ernestina Maglinao - Banca is a licensed dental professional with 25 years of experience in family and cosmetic dentistry. She is known for her gentle approach, attention to detail, and genuine care for her patients. Through continuous education and training, she remains updated with the latest advancements in dental care to provide the best service possible.

Her philosophy is simple — every patient deserves a healthy smile and a comfortable dental experience. At Maglinao-Banca Dental Clinic, she leads a team dedicated to ensuring every visit is filled with care, trust, and professionalism.
