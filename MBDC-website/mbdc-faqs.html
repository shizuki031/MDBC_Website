Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our online form on the Appointment page. We will contact you to confirm.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, we accept walk-ins if slots are available. However, we still recommend booking ahead.

What dental services do you offer?

We offer general dentistry, cleaning, extractions, whitening, and braces adjustment.

How often should I visit the dentist?

It's best to visit every 6 months for check-ups and cleaning.

Do you accept dental insurance or HMO?

Yes, we accept select insurance and HMO providers. Please bring your card for verification.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

For emergencies like pain or broken teeth, call our clinic hotline immediately.
